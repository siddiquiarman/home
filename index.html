What Is FIFA 20? (Introduction)

FIFA 20 is a soccer simulation video game published by Electronic Arts as part of the FIFA series. It is the 27th installment in the FIFA series, and was released on 27 September 2019 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch. This year it has a lot of new features being added into the game. There is brand new licensing, new stadiums, new teams being added in, and brand new player face scanning.

Fifa 20 is a very complex video game, we will be referring to the 4 main modes and giving information about what is added into them this year. Starting off with Pro clubs which most FIFA 20 players enjoy, then moving on to Gameplay, Pro clubs, Career Mode, and Volta.

This year EA sports have a added three new cover stars for three new editions:

  • Eden Hazard (Standard edition)
  • Virgil Vandijk (Champions edition)
  • Zinedine Zidane (Ultimate Edition)

    • Each one of these editions have a different price, the standard edition is $79.99, the champions edition is $99.99 and the ultimate edition is $129.99. The Champions and Ultimate edition are more expensive than the standard edition because they give more FIFA points, packs and coins(currency in the game) if you purchase it.

    What's New?

    Ea Sports have added more than 30 official leagues, for instance La Liga and the Premier league and over soccer 700 clubs and over 17,000 players. Included for the first time is the Romanian Liga I and its 14 other teams, as well as the UAE club, Al Ain, who were added following extensive requests from the fans in the region.

    Changes are also made to the traditional 11 on 11 mode to encourage more one-on-one scenarios and off-the-ball space creations simillar to real life. New penalty and free-kick mechanics were implemented and updates were made to the ball physics.
