Pro Clubs

What Is Pro clubs?

Pro clubs is an online mode in FIFA 20 that allows you to create a Virtual Professional player and play your preffered position on the pitch with your friends in a true representation of online soccer.You can play with up to 11 people on one team. You can customize your player using the customization features in the game with hairstyles, tatoos, kit fits, soccer cleats and much more. You will to create a club/team with your friends and customize the name stadium and jersey's. You can play with a minimum of two players on one team but the other nine will be random players controlled by the CPU using AI. You start off in Division 10 and the more matches you and your teammates win, your division ranking increases. Your player starts from 80 overall. You have to use skill points that you earn every few matches to upgrade your player's stats.

    • New match types

    There are two new match types you can play in FIFA 20 pro clubs, the first one is the Practice Match and the other is the House Rules cup.

    Practice Match is for teams to practice individually or as a team against varied AI difficulties, from beginner difficulty to legendary difficulty. You can set which tactics you want to play against such as possession based or high Pressing and what overall rating you want your AI opponents to be, from 80 rated all the way to 99 overall rated players.

    House rules cups are different competitions you can play but they all have a different objective to them, for instance the long range cup is a cup where you can score goals from outside the penalty box for it to stand for 2 goals, but if you just score from inside the box it will stand as just one . Another one is Headers and Volley's. You can only score header goals and volley goals in the game or else the goal won't count. House rules has all these types of games you can play with your friends as a cup match every day.
